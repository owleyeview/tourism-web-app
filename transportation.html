Getting Around Taniti

Public Bus

Taniti's public bus system is a convenient and affordable way to explore the island. With regular service between major tourist spots and the city center, you'll have no trouble getting around. Buses run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day.

  • Regular routes through Taniti City and Merriton Landing.
  • Affordable fares with frequent stops at popular attractions.

Rental Car

If you prefer to explore Taniti at your own pace, renting a car is a great option. Several rental agencies offer vehicles ranging from compact cars to SUVs, perfect for exploring both the city and the island's more remote attractions.

  • Car rental services available at the Taniti airport and major hotels.
  • Drivers must present a valid driver's license and insurance.
  • Recommended for visitors who want the freedom to explore remote areas like the volcano or hidden beaches.

Bike Rentals

For a more eco-friendly way to see Taniti, consider renting a bike. With flat, scenic routes around the city and beach areas, biking is a fun and healthy way to explore. Several vendors offer bike rentals by the hour or day.

  • Bikes available for rent in Taniti City and Merriton Landing.
  • Helmets are required by law and are provided with each rental.
  • Perfect for exploring the coastal areas and nearby attractions.
