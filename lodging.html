Where to Stay in Taniti

Resorts

For those seeking luxury and convenience, Taniti's resorts offer all-inclusive packages and world-class amenities. Perfect for both couples and families, our resorts provide everything you need for a relaxing stay.

  • Taniti Grand Resort: Located on the pristine shores of Yellow Leaf Bay, this resort features multiple swimming pools, beachfront dining, and a full-service spa. Family amenities include a kids' club, babysitting services, and family suites.
  • Sunset Sands Resort: Known for its breathtaking views and excellent service, Sunset Sands offers private cabanas, water sports, and nightly entertainment for guests.

Beach Houses

If you're looking for privacy and a home-away-from-home feel, Taniti's beach houses offer a perfect retreat. Ideal for families or groups, these houses provide a cozy and intimate setting while keeping you close to the island's top attractions.

  • Ocean Breeze Beach House: This spacious beachfront home is perfect for large families, offering a fully equipped kitchen, outdoor barbecue area, and direct access to the beach. Enjoy your own slice of paradise with plenty of space for the kids to play.
  • Seaside Retreat: With panoramic ocean views and modern amenities, Seaside Retreat is the perfect spot for couples or small families seeking a relaxing getaway.

Bed & Breakfast

For a more personal touch, our charming bed and breakfast options allow you to experience Taniti's warm hospitality in a relaxed, intimate setting. Wake up to the sounds of the ocean and enjoy a homemade breakfast each morning.

  • Blue Lagoon B&B: Nestled in a quiet corner of the island, this bed and breakfast offers beautifully decorated rooms, a tranquil garden, and delicious homemade meals. It's perfect for couples seeking a quiet, romantic getaway.
  • Island Escape B&B: Located just a short walk from the beach, Island Escape combines modern comfort with island charm. The hosts are known for their warmth and hospitality, making every guest feel right at home.
