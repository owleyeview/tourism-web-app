Top Taniti Attractions

Beaches

Taniti is home to some of the most beautiful beaches in the Pacific. Whether you're looking for a quiet spot to relax or a lively beach with activities for the whole family, you'll find your perfect slice of paradise here.

  • Yellow Leaf Bay: Famous for its powder-soft sand and crystal-clear waters, perfect for swimming and sunbathing.
  • Coral Cove: A must-visit for snorkeling enthusiasts, with vibrant coral reefs teeming with marine life.
  • Lagoon Beach: Known for its calm waters, making it ideal for families with young children.

Sights

Explore the natural and cultural wonders of Taniti. From hiking trails to breathtaking vistas, the island offers something new at every turn.

  • Rainforest Trails: Journey through lush tropical rainforests and discover exotic wildlife on guided or self-guided hikes.
  • Taniti Volcano: Witness the majestic beauty of the island's active volcano, offering a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to experience nature's power up close.
  • Taniti City Architecture: Wander through the capital city and admire its unique blend of traditional Tanitian architecture and modern influences.

Family Fun

Taniti is the perfect destination for families. Whether you're looking for outdoor adventures or kid-friendly activities, there's something for everyone to enjoy together.

  • Zip-lining: Soar through the treetops of Taniti's rainforest on an exhilarating zip-lining adventure the whole family will love.
  • Miniature Golf: Spend a relaxing afternoon at our family-friendly mini-golf course, with stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Taniti Arcade: Rainy day? No problem. Visit the Taniti Arcade for hours of entertainment with classic and modern games.
